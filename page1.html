About Us

 

"Leading towards a better tomorrow, our goal is to spread happiness and save humanity."
Thousands of people die daily and many families go into distress because of lack of SDP and Blood. We dream to not let go of the precious gift given by God in vain due to this scarcity and revive the smiles of those heavy hearts. Our first step towards serving society is providing an online platform that serves as an intermediate among donors, beneficiaries and blood banks. We want to make sure that every dusk turns into a beautiful dawn and the world is full of cheerfulness.
